The Ultimate Guide to Houseplant Care

Bringing greenery into your home adds beauty and freshness, but keeping houseplants healthy requires proper care. Follow these expert tips to help your plants thrive!

1. Get the Lighting Right

Not all plants need direct sunlight. Research your plant's lighting needs to find the perfect spot.

2. Avoid Overwatering

Check if your plant needs water by touching the soil or lifting the pot. A moisture meter can also help.

3. Use Rainwater When Possible

Rainwater is often better for plants than tap water. Collect rainwater for a natural watering solution.

4. Provide Fresh Air

Open a window near your plants to improve airflow and help them absorb carbon dioxide.

5. Prune for New Growth

Remove dead or yellowing leaves to promote healthy new growth.

6. Know When to Repot

If roots grow out of the pot's drainage holes, it's time for a bigger home.

7. Ensure Proper Drainage

Make sure pots have drainage holes to prevent water buildup and root rot.

8. Style with a Decorative Pot

Use a stylish outer pot to enhance your home decor while keeping your plant in a functional nursery pot.

9. Keep Leaves Dust-Free

Dust on leaves blocks light. Wipe them gently with a damp cloth to keep them clean.

10. Choose Pet-Safe Plants

Some plants are toxic to pets. Research before bringing a new plant home.

12. Find the Best Spot

Light, humidity, and temperature vary by room. Experiment with locations to find the best fit.
